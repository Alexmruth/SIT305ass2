There is an explorer by the name of Archer Bones whose plane has crashed during an expedition 
in a mountainous region and he is the only survivor. Mr. Bones is then greeted by a farmer who 
brings him into their remote village. Mr. Bones learns that the only way to leave the mountain 
is to defeat Nogard the Dragon, who guards the only exit.

Core Features:
•	Traverse through a series of caves.
•	Pick up gold which is spent on buying items to make Mr. Bones stronger.
•	Defeat monsters that stand in the way of your goals.
•	Interact with NPC's.
